Kicks and Klinks: Generals Shine at NCAA 101 Taekwondo

EAC Secures 17-Medal Haul in a Dominant Performance

As the Emilio Aguinaldo College (EAC) Jins conclude an eventful season, their "never-say-die" attitude has cemented their place as a powerhouse in collegiate martial arts. With a blend of veteran precision and fresh energy from the Junior ranks, the Generals dominated the mats at the San Andres Sports Complex, proving that their rigorous training and team brotherhood are the keys to their skyrocketing success.

Technical Mastery

The EAC Jins showcased elite Kyurogi (sparring) techniques, utilizing explosive head kicks and straight punches to bag 17 total medals, securing a massive second-place overall finish for the university.

The Junior Powerhouse

The Junior Boys division bolstered the team's future, with athletes like Omar Julpawaie and Antonio Joaquin Bautista clinching gold titles through superior footwork and calculated strikes in the final rounds.
